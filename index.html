Our Journey Through Seasons

TEDxVBIT began as a spark, an idea to bring powerful stories to our campus stage. Over the years, it has grown into a space where ideas are heard, challenges are questioned and voices truly matter. Whether on stage or behind a screen, we've carried the spirit through it all. Season after season, we are not just hosting events, we are building a legacy of thought, expression and change.

TED (Technology, Entertainment, and Design) is a global conference series that serves as a hub for brilliant minds and influential thought leaders from diverse fields. It provides a unique platform for these experts to come together and share their groundbreaking ideas, vast knowledge, and captivating experiences. TED Talks are short, powerful speeches on a variety of topics that aim to spread ideas and inspire people to take action in their communities. TED has become a popular platform for sharing innovative ideas, and its talks have been viewed by millions of people worldwide.

TEDx events are independently organized events that follow the same format and spirit as TED events. They are organized by local communities and individuals passionate about sharing ideas and bringing people together in their communities. TEDx events feature a combination of live speakers and TED Talk videos, and they cover a wide range of topics that are relevant to their local communities. TEDx events are licensed by TED, which provides guidelines and support to ensure that they meet the standards of quality and integrity that TED is known for.

TEDxVBIT is an independently organized TEDx event held annually at Vignana Bharathi Institute of Technology (VBIT), Ghatkesar, Hyderabad. The aim of TEDxVBIT is to inspire, educate, and empower individuals to take action and make a positive impact in their communities. The event brings together a diverse group of speakers and performers from various fields, including technology, entertainment, design, science, and art, who share their innovative ideas and experiences with the audience. Since its inception, TEDxVBIT has been among the premier TEDx experiences in Hyderabad, attracting a large and enthusiastic audience every year.
